Whether you're returning after years away or stepping onto a floor for the first time — ballet, jazz, heels, contemporary, hip hop. Drop in or commit to perform.

View Adult Schedule →
Metro Atlanta Dance strives to provide excellent, inclusive, and accessible dance education to dancers of all ages and ability in our community. We provide a community of teachers and dancers that encourage every student to try new styles, reach for improvement, and commit to powerful growth and performance.

— Metro Atlanta Dance
